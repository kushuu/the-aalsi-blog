What is this blog about?

Literally any curious soul out there who wants to learn :)

This blog is divided into several tags like Competitive Programming, Machine Learning, Cyber Security and a general tag for all those articles which don't fall under those categories.

All the tags are self exaplanatory except the last one. That segment would be having articles on trivial topics and some might even be on philosophy.

This blog will be consisting of articles explaining new projects/concepts that I work on/learn during my learing jouney.

Why this blog?

What knowledge is good if not shared with others?

Through the years of learning and practising coding related subjects, one thing that I've realised is that making projects is a good way to get actual knowledge, but to retain it for a longer time, you need to document it, mannerly. Wrtiting articles is one of the proven efficient ways to achieve this.

Penning something down makes you go through the process thoroughly, once again. Another very important aspect of writing articles (maintaining a blog) is that you have a beautiful proof of you knowing that concept.
